Altitude → Color Mapping

Every aircraft's color encodes its altitude. At a glance, see which flights are cruising at 40,000 ft and which are on approach — no label needed.

0 – 8,000 ft — Cyan (approach / departure)
8,000 – 20,000 ft — Blue (climb)
20,000 – 30,000 ft — Purple (cruise transition)
30,000 – 40,000 ft — Amber (cruise)
40,000 ft+ — Gold (high cruise)
